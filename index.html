HOW TO DELEGATE TO THE MARIDIA POOL

INSTALL

Download and install Daedalus or Yoroi wallets.

CREATE

Create or restore a wallet and send your funds from your “Exchange” of choice to the newely created wallet.

DELEGATE

Under the delegation tab, go to “Stake pools”. Search for the Maridia [MARID] pool and then click on “Delegate to this pool”.

DONE

That's it. All rest to do is to leave your funds to grow over time.
